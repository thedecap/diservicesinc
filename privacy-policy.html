Privacy Policy

DI Services Inc. Website Privacy Policy

The Internet is a powerful tool that continues to transform our lives. With just a few clicks, you can access news, shop online, connect with others globally, and more. At DI Services Inc., we are committed to protecting your privacy while you enjoy the benefits of the Internet.

We value your privacy and consider your personal information to be yours alone. That's why we are committed to safeguarding any data you share with us. DI Services Inc. adheres to globally recognized standards for customer privacy and data protection.

  • Your personal information, such as your name, address, phone number, and email, will never be sold or shared.
  • We use advanced security measures to prevent unauthorized access to your data.

Notice

We will inform you when we need personal information that can identify you or be used to contact you. Typically, this information is collected when you register, download software, subscribe to newsletters, enter contests, or access premium content. This data is used to:

  1. Simplify your experience by reducing repetitive data entry.
  2. Help you quickly access relevant services and content.
  3. Customize content based on your preferences.
  4. Notify you of updates, offers, and new services from DI Services Inc.

Consent

You can browse most of our website without registering. However, registration may be required to access certain features.

When registering, you can choose which communications you receive from us. If you prefer not to receive promotional messages, you may opt out at any time.

Access

We provide ways for you to ensure your personal information is accurate and up to date. At our Visitor Center, you can:

  • Review and update your personal data.
  • Choose whether to receive marketing communications.
  • Sign up for newsletters.
  • Register once and access our site seamlessly in the future.

Security

DI Services Inc. employs robust measures to protect your data and respects your preferences for its use. We ensure your data is safeguarded from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

Your transactions are secured with SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), a leading encryption technology, ensuring your information is safe during transfer.

Internally, we restrict data access with password-protected servers. Your information may be stored or processed in Canada or other locations where DI Services Inc. operates.

To help maintain your privacy, never share your username or password with others.

Notice to Parents

We encourage parents and guardians to guide their children in using the Internet safely and responsibly.

While our content isn't directed at children, we offer a Kids account that allows parents to consent to the collection and use of information from children under 12.

Enforcement

If you believe DI Services Inc. has not followed this policy, please email us at privacy@diservicesinc.com. Be sure to include "Privacy Policy" in the subject line. We will work promptly to address and resolve your concerns.

Electronic Product Registration

When registering a product, we merge the details with your existing profile or create a new one. You can update your profile anytime by visiting the Profile Center.

If you're new to registration, we'll help you create a Registration ID to secure your information.

Customer Profiles

Every registered customer receives a unique ID and profile to ensure your data remains secure and accessible only by you.

We place a unique cookie on your device for seamless access to our site and services. This allows you to access premium content without re-entering information, even when switching devices.

How We Use Your Information

When you register, we collect your contact details to send updates, conduct surveys, and inform you about new services. When placing an order, we collect your billing information solely for payment purposes. This data is stored securely and used only with your permission.

We occasionally partner with other companies to provide services such as shipping, answering queries, and processing event registrations. These partners only receive the information necessary to perform their roles and are prohibited from using it for other purposes.

DI Services Inc. may disclose your personal information if legally required or when necessary to:

  1. Comply with legal processes.
  2. Protect the rights or property of DI Services Inc.
  3. Act in urgent situations to ensure the safety of individuals or the public.
